# Privacy Notes

ClearMatch deliberately avoids social-network mechanics.

- No public comments.
- No follower counts.
- No reposting.
- No public feed.
- No mutual-friend display.
- No social media linking requirement.
- No public profile activity stream.

Messaging unlocks only after a mutual match. Likes, passes, standout likes, reports, blocks, hidden profile state, and paused account state are private user actions.
